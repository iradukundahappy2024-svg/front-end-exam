Who We Are

Building Tomorrow's
Rwanda, Today

Youth Empowerment Rwanda (YER) is a community-driven non-governmental organization dedicated to unlocking the full potential of young Rwandans aged 15–35. Founded in 2015, we operate across all 30 districts of Rwanda.

We believe that when young people are equipped with the right tools and opportunities, they don't just change their own lives — they transform entire communities.
