Program 01

Digital Skills Hub

Our Digital Skills Hub is a 6-month intensive training program preparing youth for careers in the digital economy. From beginners to advanced learners, we have tracks for everyone.

Participants learn through hands-on projects, industry mentorship, and real-world assignments with partner companies. Upon graduation, participants receive a recognized certification.

Program 02

Leadership Academy

Our flagship Leadership Academy is a six-month transformative program for 100 exceptional young Rwandans each year, aged 18–30, who demonstrate the potential and commitment to lead their communities.

Graduates go on to lead NGOs, start companies, win elections, and drive systemic change across Rwanda and East Africa.
